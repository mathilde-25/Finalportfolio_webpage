Hi ! My name is Mathilde, I am a first year Digital Media student at UWE. My course studies Graphic and Web Design, Media Studio, Creative Coding and Principles of 3D Environments.

With my sisters already in the UX field, I took some LinkedIn courses, which sparked a strong interest in web design and UX. That curiosity is what led me to this course !

Within this module, we were tasked to develop three graphic compositions based off the Gestalt Principles. My chosen three were; Repetition, Radial and Pattern (in order of piece). These were made through Figma.

I wanted to create a central focal point with the eye. Keeping a greyscale background so that the users eyes automatically focus towards the centre.
