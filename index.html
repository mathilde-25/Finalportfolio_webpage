Hi ! My name is Mathilde, I am a first year Digital Media student at UWE. My course studies Graphic and Web Design, Media Studio, Creative Coding and Principles of 3D Environments.

I am originally from a countryside town in Devon, where I was constantly surrounded by nature. Those landscapes taught me to appreciate the little things - how small details come together to create something beautiful.

I began as an apprentice welder and even considered tattooing—careers that all connected back to art and self-expression. With my sisters already in the UX field, I took some LinkedIn courses, which sparked a strong interest in web design and UX. That curiosity is what led me to this course !

I’ve been drawing for as long as I can remember. My art grew with me, drawing whatever passionate me through my stages of life. Traditional drawing trained my eye for detail, and that focus is something I strive to bring into all my work.
